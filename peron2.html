Hannah Gebroers

31 year old Leuvenaar with a love for biking. Two years ago i took over my family's bike shop. When I'm not running the shop or repairing bikes, I'm usually going for a bike ride or hanging out at a repair café!

Favourite repair tool?

In the bike shop I always use my dad's old repair kit. It's been in my family for 2 generations and still in excellent shape!

Your oldest memory related to repairing?

For as long as I can remember I have spent weekends in my family's bike shop.

Number 1 repair tip

Don't be afraid to repair something by yourself. Ask for help from friends or use Google. You are a lot more handy than you think you are!

Hannah Gebroers

Owner of bike repair shop
